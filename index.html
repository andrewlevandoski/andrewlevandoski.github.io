About me

I am a graduate student at the University of Pittsburgh, pursuing my M.S. in computer science. I am passionate about fusing experiences in software development and algorithm design to solve complex problems. Further, I aim to draw upon studies in economics and finance to generate data-intensive insights.

Outside of my studies I am an avid cyclist, competing with the Pitt cycling team. I also spend my time painting and taking pictures.
